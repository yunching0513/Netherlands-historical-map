歷史地圖

荷蘭歷史地形圖（1815–2021）由荷蘭土地測量局 Kadaster「Topotijdreis（時光地圖）」計畫提供，圖磚由 Esri Nederland 託管於 ArcGIS Online。內含系列：地形軍事圖（TMK，1:50.000）、Bonnebladen（1:25.000 套色地形圖）與地形圖（1:25.000）。授權：CC BY 4.0。

底圖

  • 街道圖／灰階圖：BRT Achtergrondkaart © Kadaster，透過 PDOK 提供，授權 CC BY 4.0。
  • 空照圖：Luchtfoto © Beeldmateriaal Nederland，透過 PDOK 提供，開放資料。

技術

地圖引擎 Leaflet、座標轉換 proj4js（均為開源軟體）。

謹向上述機構與所有測繪、數位化、開放這些圖資的人致謝。本站僅作非營利之教育與文化用途；著作權與相關權利歸原權利人所有。

English

Historical maps

Historical topographic maps of the Netherlands (1815–2021) are provided by the Dutch Land Registry, Kadaster, through the Topotijdreis programme, with tiles hosted by Esri Nederland on ArcGIS Online. Series included: Topographic Military Map (TMK, 1:50,000), Bonnebladen (1:25,000 chromotopographic) and Topographic map (1:25,000). Licence: CC BY 4.0.

Base maps

  • Street / grey: BRT Achtergrondkaart © Kadaster, provided via PDOK, licensed CC BY 4.0.
  • Aerial imagery: Luchtfoto © Beeldmateriaal Nederland, provided via PDOK, open data.

Technology

Leaflet (map engine) and proj4js (coordinate transforms), both open-source.

With gratitude to these institutions and to everyone who surveyed, digitised and opened this material. This site is a non-commercial educational and cultural project; all copyrights and related rights remain with their respective owners.

Nederlands

Historische kaarten

De historische topografische kaarten van Nederland (1815–2021) zijn beschikbaar gesteld door het Kadaster via het programma Topotijdreis; de tegels worden gehost door Esri Nederland op ArcGIS Online. Reeksen: Topografische Militaire Kaart (TMK, 1:50.000), Bonnebladen (1:25.000 chromotopografisch) en Topografische kaart (1:25.000). Licentie: CC BY 4.0.

Basiskaarten

  • Kaart / grijs: BRT Achtergrondkaart © Kadaster, via PDOK, licentie CC BY 4.0.
  • Luchtfoto: Luchtfoto © Beeldmateriaal Nederland, via PDOK, open data.

Techniek

Leaflet (kaartengine) en proj4js (coördinaattransformatie), beide opensource.
